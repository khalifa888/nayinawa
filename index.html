Mai Anguwa Chain


What is Mai Anguwa Chain?

Mai Anguwa blockchain is a distributed ledger with lists of records (blocks) that are securely linked together via cryptographic hashes. MAA is the native token in Mai Anguwa Chain.
Mai Anguwa Chain is based on POV consensus algorithm.

What is mining?

Mining is the process of creating new MAA coins by solving extremely complicated math problems that verify transactions in the network. When a MAA Coin is successfully mined, the miner receives a predetermined amount of MAA.

Who developed Mai Anguwa Chain?

The network was developed by spider team lead {Mai Anguwa} lives in Nigeria.

With e-currency based on cryptographic proof, without the need to trust a third party middleman, money can be secure and transactions effortless. In Mai Anguwa Chain you own your money, government have no power against your wallet.
